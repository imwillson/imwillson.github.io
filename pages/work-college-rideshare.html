hav

Built a MEAN stack application to create a community where drivers and passengers at Binghamton University could carpool better

Used: Javascript. MongoDb, Express, Angular, Node.js

Context

I drove a lot of people back and forth between NYC and Binghamton, so I wanted to take a stab at building a platform, while learning a full-stack with my partner!

My very first hackathon that I went to alone, but found a partner to build with, Nicholas Jativa

Components

Front page indicating which school system you wanted to be a part of (in our case, Binghamton University or Stony Brook University)

Ability to retrieve users who have posted in the past and display them, with a review box

What I learned:

Organizing information with CRUD

Organizing an application's logic with controllers,modules, services, and directives. Easier to debug and figure out where to place the functions needed to operate your application

DOM manipulation, RESTful API
